# Catalyst Engine — Master Creator Agreement

**Version:** 1.0 — Effective 2026-05-14

This Master Creator Agreement ("Agreement") is between Catalyst Engine, a
limited company registered in the Republic of Ireland ("Platform"), and the
individual creator who accepts it ("Creator"). By accepting this Agreement
the Creator agrees to be bound by its terms in connection with any campaign,
brand collaboration, or content created or distributed through the Platform.

## 1. Definitions

- **Brand** means an advertiser or marketing client engaging the Platform.
- **Campaign** means a discrete brief, deliverable set, or paid engagement
  arranged through the Platform.
- **Content** means any post, video, image, audio, story, or other creative
  work the Creator produces or distributes under this Agreement.
- **Platform Fees** means the percentage retained by the Platform from each
  Campaign as disclosed in the Creator's account dashboard.

## 2. Eligibility

The Creator represents that they are at least 18 years of age, legally able
to form binding agreements in their country of residence, and have provided
true and accurate identity and tax information through the onboarding
wizard. The Platform may suspend or terminate access if any representation
is found to be inaccurate.

## 3. Content ownership and licence

The Creator retains ownership of all Content they create. By participating
in a Campaign, the Creator grants the relevant Brand a non-exclusive,
worldwide, royalty-free licence to use, reproduce, and display the Content
for the purposes set out in the Campaign brief and for the duration agreed
in that brief. The Platform receives a perpetual, royalty-free licence to
use Content for promotional purposes related to the Platform itself,
including case studies, marketing materials, and creator showcase pages.

## 4. Disclosures and compliance

The Creator agrees to comply with all applicable advertising standards in
each jurisdiction in which they distribute Content, including but not
limited to clearly labelling sponsored content with `#ad`, `#sponsored`,
or the equivalent platform-native disclosure as required by the FTC (US),
ASA (UK), AGCOM (Italy), and any other governing body. Failure to disclose
may result in immediate Campaign termination without payment.

## 5. Payments

The Platform will process payment to the Creator within thirty (30) days
of Campaign completion and Brand approval, less Platform Fees. Payments
are denominated in the currency selected by the Creator during onboarding
and are subject to the Creator's verified payout method remaining active
and tax form remaining current. Tax withholding may apply per the
Creator's residency.

## 6. Brand interactions

The Creator agrees to communicate professionally with Brands through the
Platform's messaging and approval workflows. Direct off-platform contact
during an active Campaign is discouraged and may result in Campaign
forfeiture. Disputes between the Creator and a Brand are escalated to the
Platform's support team and resolved per the Platform's published dispute
resolution process.

## 7. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement on thirty (30) days written
notice. The Platform may terminate immediately for cause, including
material breach, misrepresentation, fraud, or activity that violates
applicable law or the Platform's Acceptable Use policy. Termination does
not affect Campaign deliverables in progress at the time of termination,
which complete or unwind per the Campaign brief.

## 8. Limitation of liability

The Platform's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed
the total Campaign fees paid to the Creator in the twelve (12) months
preceding the event giving rise to the claim. The Platform is not liable
for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

## 9. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of Ireland. Any
dispute that cannot be resolved through the Platform's dispute resolution
process is subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Irish courts.

## 10. Acceptance

The Creator's acceptance of this Agreement — whether through the e-sign
flow with our verification partner or through the click-through
acceptance available in the onboarding wizard — constitutes a binding
electronic signature with the same legal effect as a handwritten
signature. The Creator may request a PDF copy of the accepted Agreement
from the Platform's support team at any time.
